hi, i'm jackie!

i am an artist-at-heart, a taurus, and a current master's student at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program. previously, i worked as a product designer at Codecademy and Girls Who Code, and was a computer science student at MIT.

i'm particularly interested in the potential for design and art to examine, educate, and propose how computing culture can be more equitable.

as a designer within ed-tech, i've designed around making gate-keeped fields like programming accessible and human. as an artist, i'm interested in co-opting technological processes to carve a space for radical vulnerability and femininity within a world that does not traditionally offer room for these things.

i'm constantly exploring what kinds of things i want to create and how i want to exist. send me a message if you'd like to collaborate!
